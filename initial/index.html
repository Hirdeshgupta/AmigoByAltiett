INTRODUCING AMIGO APP
Your virtual companion with you, by you, for you, always. Amigo acts as the one-stop platform at the time of emergency.

In
just three long taps
you can

1.
Alert app users within 5 km
distance and your contacts
about the impending danger.

2.
Get a group created of the app users nearby and your favorite contacts, so that they can together decide a rescue plan for you.

3.
Automatically share your live location details and profile details (name, age, profile pic, for the ease of identification) to all your groups.

4.
Can send voice and video messages with a single tap to all your groups.

5.
Search for nearby police stations, hospitals, their contact numbers, and routes to safer places in one click.

DON'T FORGET TO
Always test run the app with your family members to understand it's working. Add your dear ones(family members, friends, relatives) to the app, send them friend requests, add them to your contacts, form their groups, choose favorite contacts and test run the app.
